    Media Cataloguer

    Data Crow is the cataloguer for software, movies, music, books and images. Any media type such as mp3, divx, xvid, ape, ogg, pdf, avi can be imported within Data Crow and further enriched using online services.

    Software

    Use online services such as Amazon.com to find information on your software and games. Retrieved information includes an in depth description of the software itself, information on who developed and who published the software, the release date, the platform on which the software runs, covers and screenshots and the average rating.

    The information can easily be retrieved and personalised. Besides the internet information you can enter extra information like software type and location. And much more!

    Music

    Data Crow contains two separate modules for cataloging music collections, the Music Album and Audio CD module. Use one of the online services (MusicBrainz, Amazon, Discogs and others) to find information on your CD and or music files. Parse information from your mp3, flac, ape and or ogg file and fill missing information using an online service.
    You can even play the music file from within Data Crow using your favorite audio player!

    Books

    Search for your titles by using one of the online services such as Amazon.com, Bol.com, Barnesandnoble.com. Data Crow retrieves descriptions, information about the author, year of release, average user rating, a front cover picture and more! Just like with all the other modules you can easily retrieve and change this information.
    The information can be exported to a pdf or html file for nice print-outs

    Movies

    The Imdb database is perhaps one of the largest and most reliable sources for movie descriptions available on the web. Data Crow uses this gigantic database and others to retrieve information on the movies you own.
    Information retrieved includes the cast, director, the country in which the movie was made, year of release, language, certification, a rating and much more.
    Data Crow is capable of extracting information from all the common movie file formats (divx and such). Information on codecs, resolution and other technical information is imported into Data Crow.

    Images

    Keep track of your images. Be they JPEG, GIF or PNG files, Data Crow will read the information stored within these images. Data Crow can extract the date on which the image was created, technical information such as compression, height and width and which (if applicable) camera was used to take the photograph. To easily retrieve your images you can add a description and a name to each image. Beside this information you categorize the images and assign (definable) states.
