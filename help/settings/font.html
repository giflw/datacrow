Fonts

Within Data Crow two different fonts are used, fonts for displaying text and fonts used for labels and buttons (etc). You can select the font type, size and style (bold and or italic). An sample of the selected settings is shown on lower part. Once you are happy with your settings press "save" to apply them.

At any time you press "reset". The font settings will default to the defaults of your system. To apply the values press "save".

Anti-aliasing

In case all fonts are shown incorrectly on your screen you can try to solve this by enabling font anti-aliasing. Just check the "Font anti-aliasing" box and press "save".
